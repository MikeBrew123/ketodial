05 Medication considerations
MedicationConsideration on a ketogenic dietRisk
MetforminGenerally compatible. Watch for additive GI upset early on; lactic acidosis remains rare. No routine dose change purely for diet.lo
Insulin / sulfonylureas
if applicable		Highest hypoglycemia risk with carb restriction. Typically requires pre-emptive dose reduction and glucose monitoring.hi
SGLT2 inhibitors
if applicable		Small but real risk of euglycemic diabetic ketoacidosis when combined with ketogenic eating. Discuss before starting.hi
ACE inhibitors / ARBs
e.g. lisinopril		Blood pressure may decline with sodium/water loss. Monitor for lightheadedness; clinician may reduce dose.med
Diuretics
if applicable		Keto itself is diuretic. Combined effect may cause dehydration and electrolyte depletion. Close monitoring advised.med
