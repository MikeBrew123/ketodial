Calculator Estimates

The calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, which is a well-established method for estimating basal metabolic rate. It's an estimate. Your actual caloric needs may differ based on factors the calculator can't measure, like metabolic adaptation, muscle mass, hormonal status, and genetics.

The macro ratios we apply (roughly 70% fat, 25% protein, 5% net carbs) are standard ketogenic targets. They are not guaranteed to produce ketosis for every person, and individual results will vary.
